## 1. Mortgage Products (Hypotheken)

Raiffeisen is Switzerland's largest mortgage lender. Each of the ~220 local Raiffeisen banks sets its own rates; the figures below are national averages from VZ VermögensZentrum (17 Mar 2026).

### 1.1 Festhypothek (Fixed-Rate Mortgage)

| Term | Indicative Rate (avg) |
|------|----------------------|
| 2 years | 1.32% |
| 3 years | ~1.40% (interpolated) |
| 5 years | 1.61% |
| 7 years | ~1.80% (interpolated) |
| 10 years | 1.98% |
| 15 years | 2.36% |

- **Terms**: 2 to 15 years, freely selectable
- **Features**: Fixed rate for entire term, full payment predictability
- **Terminhypothek** (forward mortgage) available: lock in rate in advance for a surcharge
- **No early termination** during term
- **No model switch** during term
- **Amortization**: 2nd mortgage must be amortized to 65% LTV within 15 years (or by retirement)

### 1.2 SARON Flex-Hypothek (Money Market Mortgage)

- **Rate composition**: Compounded SARON base rate + individual credit margin (fixed for term)
- **Current SARON**: approximately -0.04% (SNB at 0% since Jun 2025)
- **Typical all-in rate**: ~0.76--1.00% (SARON + margin of 0.80%+)
- **Floor**: If SARON goes negative, base rate floors at 0%; only margin applies
- **Available since**: April 2020 (replaced LIBOR)
- **One-time switch**: Can switch to another mortgage model once during term
- **Interest calculation**: Retroactive, based on daily SARON compounding

### 1.3 Variable Hypothek

- **Rate**: 2.91% (average, Mar 2026) -- significantly higher than fixed/SARON
- **Notice period**: 6 months (may vary by bank)
- **Features**: No fixed term, rate adjusts with market, can switch models anytime
- **Use case**: Short-term bridging, planned property sale, maximum flexibility

### 1.4 General Mortgage Conditions

- **Loan-to-value**: Max 80% (1st mortgage to 65%, 2nd mortgage 65--80%)
- **Affordability**: Imputed rate ~5% + amortization + ancillary costs must be < 33% of gross income
- **Pillar 3a / PK withdrawal**: Allowed for owner-occupied property purchase
- **Zinsalarm**: Free email alert when rates hit your target (raiffeisen.ch feature)

## 2. Vorsorge & Insurance (Pension / Retirement)

### 2.1 Säule 3a (Pillar 3a -- Tax-Advantaged Retirement)

**Maximum contributions 2026:**
- Employed with pension fund: CHF 7'258/year
- Self-employed (no PK): 20% of net income, max CHF 36'288/year
- **NEW 2026**: Retroactive catch-up contributions allowed for gaps from 2025 onward (up to 10 years back)

#### Vorsorgekonto 3a (Savings Account)
- Interest rate: **0.200%**
- Account management: Free
- Withdrawal notice: 31 days (2% penalty for early access)
- Risk-free, capital guaranteed

#### Vorsorgefonds (Pension Funds) -- 4 Strategies

| Strategy | Equity Allocation | Risk Profile |
|----------|------------------|--------------|
| **Ertrag** (Yield) | ~25% | Conservative |
| **Ausgewogen** (Balanced) | ~45% | Moderate |
| **Wachstum** (Growth) | ~65% | Dynamic |
| **Aktien** (Equity) | ~95-100% | Aggressive |

**Fund product lines:**
- **Raiffeisen Futura -- Pension Invest** (actively managed): Yield, Balanced, Growth, Equity
- **Raiffeisen Futura II -- Systematic Invest** (index-based): Yield, Balanced, Growth, Equity

**Fund fees:**
- Purchase commission: 0.75% on first CHF 100'000; 0.15% above
- 31-day waiting period before initial investment

#### Digitale Säule 3a
- Open account in e-banking in ~3 minutes, fully paperless
- Same fund strategies as above
- Invest directly from e-banking without branch visit

### 2.2 Freizügigkeitskonto (Vested Benefits Account)

- Interest rate: **0.050%**
- Account management: Free
- Withdrawal notice: 31 days
- Fund investment option available (same Futura fund range)
- Fund commission: 0.75% (first 100k) / 0.15% (above)
- **Restricted account**: Only accepts transfers from pension funds (no voluntary deposits)
- Withdrawal only for: property purchase, self-employment, emigration, disability, near retirement (5yr window)

### 2.3 Insurance Products (Säule 3b)

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Erwerbsunfähigkeits-Rente** | Disability income insurance, regular pension payments |
| **Todesfall-Versicherung** | Death benefit / life insurance, immediate payout |
| **Kapital-Lebensversicherung** | Endowment life insurance, guaranteed capital, tax-free income |
| **Amortisations-Versicherung** | Mortgage protection insurance (covers debt on death/disability) |
| **Haushaltsversicherung** | Household / contents insurance |
| **Sparzielabsicherung** | Savings goal protection for 3a contributions |

## 7. Key Differentiators for Demo Narrative

1. **Cooperative model**: Raiffeisen is a cooperative -- customers become members (Genossenschafter) by buying a share certificate (CHF 200-500). This grants voting rights, dividend, and preferred rates.
2. **Largest mortgage lender in CH**: ~17% market share in Swiss mortgages.
3. **3rd largest banking group in CH**: After UBS and Zürcher Kantonalbank.
4. **~220 independent banks**: Each sets own rates/fees within Raiffeisen framework.
5. **1'500 ATMs**: Largest ATM network in Switzerland.
6. **Futura sustainability brand**: All investment products carry ESG/sustainability label.
7. **Raiffeisen Rio**: Digital wealth management from CHF 5'000 -- competitive entry point.
8. **Digitale Säule 3a**: Fully digital pension opening -- no branch visit needed.
9. **Regional roots**: Strong in rural/suburban Switzerland, trusted local bank positioning.
10. **SNB at 0%**: Current zero-rate environment makes SARON mortgages very attractive (~0.8% all-in).
